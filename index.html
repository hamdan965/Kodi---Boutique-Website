The Tale Of Kodi.....

The Kodi story initiated upon its journey a decade ago when I wondered at my best friend's transition from an engineering career to fashion design. She dedicated herself in curating handcrafted ethnic dresses for the Indian community in the United States. Our shared love for handloom and handcrafted designs influenced Kodi's creation . Kodi, at its core, is a manifestation of nostalgia and an unwavering love for artistry and handwoven textiles. Our vision is to make modern ethnic wear readily accessible to the next generation of women. Kodi stands firmly upon the design principles of comfort, quality, and minimalism, ensuring that every product bearing the Kodi brand exemplifies these core values.
